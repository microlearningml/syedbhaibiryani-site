Our Menu

“The Heart of Syed Bhai Biryani”
Prepared using the traditional dum method over natural firewood.

Our Signature Biryani

Our signature biryanis are prepared using the traditional dum method over natural firewood, allowing every grain of basmati rice to absorb the rich aroma of carefully selected spices.

Chicken Biryani ⭐ Signature

Our most loved dish. Tender halal chicken is marinated with aromatic spices before being layered with premium basmati rice and slow-cooked over firewood. Every serving delivers authentic wedding-style flavor, rich aroma, and perfectly balanced spices.

Chicken 65 Biryani ⭐ Customer Favourite

A delicious combination of our crispy signature Chicken 65 blended with fragrant dum biryani rice. Perfect for those who enjoy bold flavors and a spicy twist on traditional biryani.

Egg Biryani

Simple, satisfying, and full of flavor. Fragrant dum-cooked basmati rice served with a perfectly cooked masala egg, making it an ideal choice for egg lovers.

Pulav with Dhalcha

Soft white basmati pulav paired with traditional dhalcha. A comforting combination that offers light flavors while maintaining authentic traditional taste.

Chef’s Specials

“Freshly Prepared. Perfectly Seasoned.” Our chef’s special dishes are prepared fresh throughout the day using carefully selected ingredients and signature spice blends.

Chicken 65 🔥

Crispy outside. Juicy inside. Boneless halal chicken marinated in our signature spices and fried until golden perfection. A perfect companion to biryani or a delicious snack on its own.

Kaada 65 (Quail Fry)

A true delicacy for meat lovers. Whole quail is marinated with traditional spices and fried until beautifully crisp while remaining tender and juicy inside. Rich in flavor and loved by those looking for something unique.

Egg Masala

Perfectly boiled eggs simmered in a rich onion, tomato, and traditional spice gravy. An excellent side dish that pairs beautifully with biryani, rice, or pulav.
