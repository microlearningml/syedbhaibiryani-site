How to Order

We welcome you for dine-in, takeaway and bulk / party orders.

  • Dine-in — Visit us any day between 11:00 AM and 10:30 PM.
  • Takeaway / Parcel — Call ahead or walk in. Fresh parcels available throughout operating hours.
  • Family Functions & Catering — We accept bulk and party orders. Please contact us in advance so we can prepare the perfect quantity with the same authentic taste.
